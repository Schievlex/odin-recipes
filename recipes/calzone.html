Calzone recipe

Image: Calzone freshly prepared, from the oven

Description

A baked Italian turnover made of pizza dough and stuffed with tomato, cheese and other toppings

Ingredients

  • pizzadeeg
  • tomatensaus
  • rode ui
  • mozarella
  • kaas

Steps

  1. preheat the oven 200 degrees
  2. roll the deeg with a deegroller
  3. spread the tomato sauce on the calzone
  4. cut the onions and spread on the calzone
  5. put the kaas on the calzone
  6. put the mozarella on the calzone
  7. put in the preheated oven for 12 min
  8. Enjoy!
