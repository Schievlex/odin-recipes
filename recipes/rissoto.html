Risotto

Image: Risotto Tomato freshly prepared, from the oven

Description

The risotto tomato is a popular risoto dish in Italy. Italians love risotto and especially love risotto tomato. Tomato works great on a risotto.

Ingredients

  • risottorijst
  • tomatensaus
  • rode ui
  • mozarella
  • tomaten

Steps

  1. make 1 liter vegetable bouillon
  2. put risotto rice in pan
  3. put a little bit of the boullion in the pan till it soaked up by the rice
  4. spread the tomato sauce on the risotto
  5. cut the onions and spread on the risotto
  6. put the mozarella on the risotto
  7. taste risotto till its not crisp anymore but still firm
  8. Bon appetite
