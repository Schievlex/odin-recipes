Pizza Tonno recipe

Image: Pizza tonno freshly prepared, from the oven

Description

The Pizza Tonno is a popular Pizza in Italy. Italians love fish and especially love Tuna. Tuna works great on a pizza.

Ingredients

  • pizzadeeg
  • tomatensaus
  • rode ui
  • mozarella
  • tonijn

Steps

  1. preheat the oven 200 degrees
  2. roll the deeg with a deegroller
  3. spread the tomato sauce on the pizza
  4. cut the onions and spread on the pizza
  5. put the tuna on the pizza
  6. put the mozarella on the pizza
  7. put in the preheated oven for 12 min
  8. Enjoy!
